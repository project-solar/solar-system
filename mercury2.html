MERCURY

  • Mercury is the closest planet to the sun but yet not the hottest. Sometimes, it can get really cold in mercury as it lacks a real atmosphere. Due to this, the surface of mercury isn't protected from asteroids and meteorites.
  • The temperature of a day in mercury can reach up-to 430 degree Celsius and can drop to 180 degree Celsius during a night
  • Distance from Sun: 57.91 million km
  • Orbital period: 88 days
  • Length of day: 58d 15h 30m
  • Radius: 2,439.7 km
  • Mass: 3.285 × 10^23 kg (0.055 M⊕)
  • A day in mercury is equal to 58 days, 15 hours and 30 minutes of the earth
